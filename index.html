Apex Legends

Description

Apex legends is a battle royale/shooter game with lots of characters. Each character has his own abilities and so his own playstyle, there are around 20 characters. Every season a new legend will be added. I like Apex so much because of the fact that it is very different than other shooting games such as Call Of Duty, Fortnite, Valorant,...

    • Heirlooms

    That one thing everyone grinds for, Heirlooms! If u play a game, u earn xp and if u earn enough, you level up. There are ofcourse awards, every 2-5 levels u earn an Apex Pack. These contain items such as skins for weapons, craftingmaterials,... But there is one thing every player hopes to pack all the time, Heirloom shards. You have a little chance to pack these, 0.2% chance! With these shards you can craft an heirloom. Every heirloom belongs to a legend, so if u play with wraith all the time, u will probably buy her kunai knife if u get the chance.
